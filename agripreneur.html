Saveda Farm started its operation in 2018. Brought as barren land, today Saveda farm owns more than 200 varieties of herbs and medicinal plants and more than 30 varieties of trees. The farm also includes a “Gosala” with fifty indigenous variety of Gir Cows. The farm today produces organics vegetables and fruits apart from supplying Pure Gir Cow Milk and Ghee to urban India.

His concept of “Gosala” is based on Vedic period. Cows are allowed to gaze in natural habitat so as to ensure that they are healthy. Farms follows natural breeding process against injection-based breeding. Cow milk is collected , bottled , chilled and supplied to the customers without artificial process like pasteurisation.Ghee at Saveda farms are made through five step processes including boiling of milk, curdling, churning, separating makkhan and finally heating of makkhan to get pure ghee
