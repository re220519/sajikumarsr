Sajikumar Nair, hail from Vaikom of Kerala , is one of the very few successful migrated south Indian entrepreneurs. Since younger age, he has been working hard to live as his father passed away at early age He had a heart for taking up any job that came to him and stood ready to go through every adverse working condition His migration to Mumbai, at the age of 19, was a turning point in his life.

In 2005, he set out his own venture namely “Afford Logistics Pvt Ltd” . Today Afford Group consists of more than six companies.

In 2018 he became a farmer by owning farm land at Raigad District of Maharastra. Subsequently “SIIMSAR – Saveda Institute of Indigenous Medical Science and Research” was born with a motto of “providing superior lifestyle to people”. Today SIIMSAR is a research based Ayurvedic institution which has already provided overwhelmingly cheerful recovery experience to many patients, who had suffered worsening health conditions for many years even after expensive treatment elsewhere.

In 2019 he penned his first book namely “By Heart” which holds an exceptional perspective on life.

He can be reached at saji@afford.co.in

“ The ultimate aim of SIIMSAR and SAVEDA is to build an eco-system benefiting both urban class through superior lifestyle and farmers with better returns . SIIMSAR provides authentic Ayurvedic treatment based on quality herbs collected from farmers across India, giving them a guaranteed return for their cultivation. SAVEDA provides quality products to the urban India through the hard work of farmers and adivasis, which provides them a guaranteed income”
