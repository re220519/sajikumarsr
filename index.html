“NATURE IS YOUR NECESSITY,
NOT THE OTHER WAY ROUND”

- S K

A passionate entrepreneur and strong lover of nature, Sajikumar dreams about a life where conflicts between humankind and nature are minimum. He strappingly believes that nature is the inevitable part of humankind, not the other way round. Nature is within us and around us. We need to consider our life as a cyclic journey within nature. When we forget the inevitability of nature in our life, the end of humankind begins.
His social interventions through Ayurveda and Writing is aimed towards the goal of reducing conflicts between humankind and nature, and helping people to improve their quality of life.
